·         Right to rectification: You have the right to request us to modify your personal information to be accurate, up-to-date, complete, and not misleading.

·         Right to file a complaint: You have the right to file a complaint to a competent official under the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 anytime we violate or do not comply with the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562.
